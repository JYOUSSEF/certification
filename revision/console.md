## Niveaux de verbosité de la sortie

- `VERBOSITY_NORMAL` (default)
- `VERBOSITY_VERBOSE` (`-v`)
- `VERBOSITY_VERY_VERBOSE` (`-vv`)
- `VERBOSITY_DEBUG` (`-vvv`)
